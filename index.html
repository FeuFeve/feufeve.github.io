TERogue

Type: game
Language: C# (Unity)
Year: 2021

You're heading into a seemingly endless dungeon, infested with monstrosities and enemies of all kinds. Your most faithful companion: the merchant. In exchange for a few gold coins collected from the corpses of your victims, he will be able to sell you a few trinkets that could be very useful. Beware, the owners of the place are notorious for not being kind to visitors.

Too cowardly to venture out alone? Use the 2-player mode and bring a friend along to be your cannon fodder! Fun guaranteed, satisfied or buried!

You'll be pitted against other players on a scoreboard of the best explorers! Will you be able to meet the challenge and reach the top of the podium?
